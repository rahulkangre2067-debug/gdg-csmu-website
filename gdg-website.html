// About Us

We are GDG CSMU.

Building the future, one developer at a time.

GDG CSMU is the official Google Developer Group chapter at Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj University, Shedung, Panvel. We bring together students, developers, and tech enthusiasts to learn, build, and grow.

From hands-on workshops to annual DevFests, we create opportunities for every student at CSMU to connect with the global Google developer ecosystem.
